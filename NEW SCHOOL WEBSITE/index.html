Welcome to S K PUBLIC SCHOOL

S.K. Public School established in 2006, is run and managed by Sabuj Kalyan Sangha. The School will be affiliated with ICSE. It is spread over 4 acres of land and has a magnificent building for students with attractive gardens and vast playgrounds. The layout and design of the new school compound has allowed the school to function in a unique way. It has enabled the school to install areas and spaces that nurture young talents by encouraging observation, practice and participation in a variety of different fields.

ABOUT

S K PUBLIC SCHOOL

Situated in the heart of Telco Colony, S.K. Public School has grown tremendously from the past nine years. It has been converted to an English Medium School following ICSE methods of teaching. This School basically caters to the economically weaker sections of society. Our vision is to provide a happy, caring and stimulating environment where children achieve their fullest potential, so that they can make their best contribution to society.

Principal

In every child, there is a world waiting to be explored .We are committed in our mission to inculcate holistic education to ensure a peace loving and harmonious world. Since its inception, S.K.Public School has endeavoured to transcend learning beyond boundaries. We are devoted to set benchmarks in nurturing children to grow into strong, matured, sensible, compassionate and intelligent adults who are original in their ideas and decisive in their actions. As it is well said “Educating the mind without educating the heart is no education at all” Our approach to learning is child centric which encourages Activity based learning. We enable them to bring out their best in a progressive and competitive environment in every field be it academics, sports or co- curricular activities.
We encourage to inculcate three core values. Explore, Execute and Excel. Our vision is to provide a happy, caring and stimulating environment where children achieve their fullest potential so that they can make their best contribution to the society. The staff, students, and administration work tirelessly to make this vision a glorious reality. We ensure that the unique abilities and persona of each individual receive adequate attention to bloom uninhibited.
This approach has led S.K. PUBLIC SCHOOL to the apex of academic achievements, extra- curricular brilliance and great repute.
